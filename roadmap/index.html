    Roadmap

    Komposition 0.1.0 has just been released. Although it's very exciting, Komposition is lacking some big features. For coming releases, we're hoping to include a lot of those features.

    Have a look at the 0.2.0 milestone on GitHub to see what's planned.

    If you have any other suggestions, or want to help with these features, please head over to the Gitter room to discuss them.
